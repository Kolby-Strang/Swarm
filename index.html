GAME MECHANICS

You are a vampire and your goal is to turn all the citizens in this town into bats.

You can turn people into bats by attacking certain locations.

After each attack every bat and person will randomly pick a new location to occupy.


THE HUNTER

The hunter is a randomly chosen citizen that attempts to stop you from infecting everyone. If you attempt to turn the hunter into a bat before everyone else has turned, you lose.


THE HEALER

The healer prevents your takeover by healing bats around him, at the end of one of your attacks before the citizens shuffle around he will choose one bat within his location to heal and turn back into a human.


BATS

Bats that you make while you turn citizens will spy for you and report what they see. Any bats that are in the same location as the hunter or healer will tell you that they see them, but not where they are.
